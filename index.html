About

I am a Quantiative Researcher at Two Sigma. Previously, I was a Research Scientist at Google Research, where I worked on algorithms for efficient machine learning. I received my Ph.D. from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) under my advisor Professor Daniela Rus in the Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory (CSAIL).
