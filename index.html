Quantitative Researcher

Researching efficient algorithms for large-scale learning systems.

I'm a quantitative researcher at Two Sigma. Earlier I spent several years at Google Research building practical methods for efficient machine learning. I earned my Ph.D. at MIT with Professor Daniela Rus in the Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory (CSAIL).
